## What changed

- `lgtm init` repairs V2 configs generated by older releases that contain
  obsolete `languages` or `required_commands` fields.
- Repair removes only those two legacy fields after the remaining config passes
  the strict V2 schema.
- Unrelated unknown fields and other malformed configs remain rejected without
  mutation.
